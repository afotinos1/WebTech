About Me

⚞ Arianna Fotinos ⚟

Welcome everyone! I started this website after quarantine in Athens had me feeling like a food expert. Over the past months ordering food was as close to as “going out” as one could due to the Covid-19 restrictions but through that experience I discovered all the best restaurants operating in athens through efood. This way you can bring the restaurant to your home. So I gathered all this information and created a list of 10 different types of food one could order and selected only the very best top quality one in two different ranges. This list includes all relavent infromation :

  • Photos
  • Reviews
  • Prices
  • Contact Info
    • Name & Location

A little bit more about me is that some of these restaurants I have visited in person before covid. I am Greek-American and raised most of my life in Greece and my favorite thing about the city was the endless options of places to go out to . Combined with my love for traveling and learning about other cultures, I got more and more into trying new cuisines and learning more about their traditional food. Therefore over the years scouting for the next best taste became a passion of mine.

Let me know what your thought of the restaurants!
