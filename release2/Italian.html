#1 Restraurant

Mailo's - The Pasta Project

Mailos is a small pop-up Italian restaurant that sells only pasta. This is the only shop in Athens that originates from Italian cuisine and serves only one choice yet it has proven to be a huge success. Customers can either make their own pasta or choose from a unique set of combinations offered in their menu that are guaranteed to make you drool. For anyone having pasta cravings, this place is affordable, adorable, and delicious Learn More

#2 Restraurant

Malconi's

This is a wonderful Italian restaurant in the heart of Athens. It offers a variety of dishes including different kinds of pasta and pizza as well as some classic appetizers. What I love about this restaurant is the atmosphere and specifically their carbonara penne pasta. After trying that pasta, I always go back to the restaurant with the same order and I am never disappointed. Its definitely in the higher price range, but the food quality and presentation is reflected in the price. Love this spot! Learn More
