#1 Restraurant

Vegan Beat

Greece is still a little bit behind when it comes to offering options for vegans or vegetarians but thankfully, this restaurant saves the day a little. One could argue that they wouldn’t even be able to visually tell the difference between what they are eating and actual meat and the taste is not substituted either. This restaurant offers, fabulous vegetarian and vegan options at an affordable price and they have a large variety of dishes they offer. This makes it a little gem in the restaurant industry, especially for a category in which choices of restaurants are scarce. Vegetarian or not, this place is worth it. Learn More

#2 Restraurant

Avocado

Very cute and unique restaurant. This restaurant is a whole mood by its self. It is very laid back and comfortable. Its interior space offers a coffee shop feeling and even has an area with private rooms and the option for certain tables to sit down. This is a unique design for Greek standards as its not typical for customers to be eating while sitting on the floor. Somehow though, the atmosphere they create is very welcoming and enjoyable. At the same time, when you see the food the only thing that will come to your mind is WOW. The portions are really big but that only means that you get double as much tastiness!! Definitely recommend this place. Learn More
