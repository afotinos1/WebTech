Restaurant #2 : Fisarmonika Mezedopotion

“Mezedopolion” is a word of Greek origins which translates to “small bites”. A subcategory of the Greek traditional cuisine is “mezedes” meaning bringing out small plates of food, typically of a large variety as a companion to the drinks that being served. This style is seen all throughout taverns, islands and various restaurants in Athens but the best one out there in terms of price and taste is Fisarmonika. This restaurant has managed to take the traditional concept of serving mezedes and upgrade it through slightly larger potions, aesthetically pleasing dishes and amazing taste while remaining affordable. This is a restaurant I have taken all my friends and family to and have probably tried every single dish on the menu, because it was honestly just that good. Once you try this restaurant you will see your self coming back over and over again! Learn More
