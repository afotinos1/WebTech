#1 Restraurant

BigBadWolf

This souvlaki spot is one of Athens favorite fast-food chains with stores all over the area. Their menu offers a variety of choices for souvlakia as well as other options such as burgers salads and more. They are also known for their fast delivery service and their special sauce. Typically their stores offer in door dining and delivery through due to the current covid situation they have been doing delivery solely. Every week, through efood, they offer great combos worth seeing! Learn More
