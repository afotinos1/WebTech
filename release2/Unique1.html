Restaurant #1 : Funky Gourmet

Funky Gourmet is not just a restaurant but an experience.

Its a one of a kind contemporary restaurant thats offer very innovative, fun and unique dishes. A lot of the dishes they offer are not what they actually seem. What you see and what you taste can be completely different. Not to worry through, this is all part of a well thought out menu that uses food as an illusion for food. The restaurant is also awarded with two Michelin star an award giving to restaurants of high culinary merit. The price for the restaurant is high, 160 euros per person which makes this a special restaurant destination. Meaning, its worth trying out once with a special someone, or with your family if you would really like to try fine dining, out-side the box food. One of my personal dishes I had tried when I visited the place was a dessert that looked like a chocolate cake, yet somehow had the taste of specifically a greek salad. Its basically food science! You will definitely be left amazed and questioning life afterwards. Learn More
