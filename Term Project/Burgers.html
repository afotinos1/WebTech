#1 Restraurant

Goody's

Goody’s is one of the most well-known burger houses in Athens with locations all over. Its a chain that has proven over time that its here to stay providing customers with affordable burgers that are fulfilling. This would be considered a more “fast food” option but it not to be underestimated. The sizes of the burgers are decent and it has a lot of choices to pick from as well as a vegetarian option. Compared to other fast-food burger places in its price range it has the most fulfilling portion and taste! Learn More

#2 Restraurant

Juicy Grill

This is hands down one of my FAVORITE burger places ever. Their burgers are on the pricier side but every single penny is worth it. Once you eat a burger from here you will never want to eat from anywhere else to be honest. The burgers are not just tasty they are juicy, almost desirable. You can tell that everything is extremely fresh and the meat quality is very high end. Their fries are also very crispy and the size of the meals, even their appetizers are huge. The best thing about them is the combo of burgers they offer, no other restaurant offers the combinations they do and it has worked out for them because their food is one of the best in Athens if not Greece in my opinion.
