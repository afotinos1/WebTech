#1 Restraurant

Ancho

Looking for a cute fast food mecixan place with amazing taste. Well Anchos is the store for you! Anchos is a chain store in various locations across Athens with a very lively and happy atmosphere but its remains close to its customers throughout quarantine through its delivery service. Its menu consists of classic mexican dishes such as nachos, rice bowls, tacos, burritos, quesadillas and delicious deserts worth trying. Its worth its price for sure! Learn More

This restaurant is the place to go if your looking for a large variety of original, high quality mexican cuisine. Other than the amazing space of the restaurant and the environment they create through events and open bars they also serve gourme mexican dishes. You feel full just from looking at the plates and their desserts are to die for! This is the perfect choice for people looking into digging deep into the cuisine and trying new plates other than the classics.
