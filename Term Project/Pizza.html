#2 Restraurant

OVIO

This is a very modern and cozy restaurant that offers small fluffy delicious pizzas. Their pizza size is perfect for one person and the freshness of the ingredients is visible. Their pizza toppings and combinations are unique and very well thought out. Let’s not forget what they say “ the eye eats first” and their pizzas definitely look like a whole meal. Their menus also have a large variety of other main courses that one can look through. Its important to note that their delivery is very fast and their menus make you feel like you went out to a restaurant even though most people are enjoying their meals through delivery due to covid.
