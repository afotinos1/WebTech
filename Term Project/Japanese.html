#1 Restraurant

Koi Sushi Bar

Koi is a Japanese focused restaurant that would be considered the “fast food” version of sushi, yet due to its restaurant’s appearance, high quality and taste, customers feel like they are in a very good restaurant. The prices it has is the most reasonable prices out of all option in Athens for the taste and quality it provides. With locations all over Athens, delivery is fast and food is in your house in no time. They are the perfect way to dive into Japanese cuisine for a beginner that does not want to spend too much money or for sushi lovers that dont want to pay extreme prices for good sushi. Worth it! #2 Restraurant

Hachiko-Sushi Tales & Cocktails

High end sushi can be difficult to find in Athens especially when comparing the quality to international Japanese restaurants but Hachiko, is as close as it gets. Their sushi is served with the most amount of detail to make sure that the taste is amazing and the presentation is always fantastic. The pieces of the rolls are also slightly bigger than normal, making the meal very fulfilling for those scared that sushi cannot make them full. The quality of the fish they use is very good which is evident from the color and smell of the tuna and salmon used. Overall just an amazing store, fantastic sushi, a great menu, and wonderful service. Learn More
