What do we do?

Explore the best options for your munchies in AthensFoodGod. We provide a list of ideas and options from different cuisines and restaurants offered in Athens. For each category offered we will offer two restaurant alternatives in different price ranges because good food does not always mean expensive food. So AthensFoodGod is here for YOU to make the process of discovering new cuisines or new restaurants in Athens as simple as possible.














Covid Restrictions

With the current Covid-19 pandemic affecting small business and our daily lifes, AthensFoodGod has gathered a list of restaurants and shops that are currently still functioning yet mostly through delivery and specifically through efood. Efood delivery brings the restaurant to your house in no time.
