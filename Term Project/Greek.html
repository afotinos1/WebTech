#1 Restraurant

Kiouzin

In the center of Athens is Kiouzin which in greek stands for Kitchen. It literally brings all the traditional modern plates in Greek cuisine with a small modern twist. The location itself is ideal but the food also is surprisingly better than expected. Considering it located in between all the touristic taverns downtown, this store keeps it authentic and manages to attract customers through its low price, cute decor, and great taste. Definitely stands out!! Learn More

AMAZING. A must go. All of the greek staples reimagined by their incredible talent and mind. Attentive service, super nice ambience. The pastiscio is a must have, the bougatsa as well. COMING BACK for sure. Thank you!
Excellent restaurant! We were looking for some Greek food but not the usual classics you get everywhere - and were really blown away by Kiouzin. The menu focuses on a couple of dishes and creates a very good variation of it. Service was even better than the food. The waitress was very attentive, informative and kind. We truly felt like welcomed guests :)We will definitely come here again and recommend everyone staying in Athens do so as well.

#2 Restraurant

ALERIA

I cannot stress enough how good the food in this restaurant is. Everything about the place is just amazing. The restaurant itself is located inside an old restored villa making the atmosphere feel very elegant yet cozy which creates a perfect balance once the food comes to the table as well. They too have added their own unique twist to the traditional Greek cuisine while keeping the important origins of the dishes. The quality of the food is very high end and the price reflects that. This is a good option for anyone wanting to try a new take one Greek traditional dishes. Must try! Learn More
